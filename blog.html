Customs Duty and Clearing Charges


The customs duty and clearance fee vary, depending on the car, model and manufactured year. It is advised never to buy cars over 15 years.

Used vehicles will pay 40% duty.

Newer vehicle that are above 2016 will attract a 40% level charge and 40% duty making it a total of 80% duty.


Note: We charge 5% of the car value as our procurement cost.

These costs must be paid by individuals and businesses shipping cars in to Nigeria.

Note that our clearing agents will work to ensure your cars are cleared professionally and delivered within one week to avoid demurrage and other charges resulting from delays in clearing.
